ResBaz Arizona

The Research Bazaar is a worldwide festival promoting the digital literacy emerging at the center of modern research. Throughout 2024, events are happening at university campuses around the globe.

ResBaz Arizona is a free, multi-day intensive conference happening May 15th - 17th 2024 where researchers come together to up-skill in “next generation digital research tools and skills.” In the spirit of a marketplace or bazaar, ResBaz is a highly participatory event where researchers from many different disciplines can learn, share knowledge and skills, and have fun!

Who should attend? Researchers at ALL levels from ALL disciplines. PhD and research master’s students are especially encouraged to attend. Research technologists who support researchers to do their job should also come along!

How do I participate in ResBaz?

At ResBaz, you choose your level of participation. Join us for one session, one day, or the entire conference, depending on your interests and availability.

Want to be part of the team? On the event registration link, you will find an option to volunteer to be contacted to speak, help, or facilitate a session.

What can I do at ResBaz?

Learn, Network, Teach, Help, and Discuss are just a few of the available things to do at ResBaz. Use the conference to add organizing events to your resume or simply attend and pick up a new data science trick, the choice is all yours!

Unconference Day at ResBaz 2024

The second day of ResBaz 2024, Thursday, May 16th, is our highly interactive “Unconference Day.” This participant-driven event is a unique opportunity for attendees to shape the conference experience through their own interests and expertise.

What is an Unconference?

An unconference is a collaborative model where the attendees themselves propose and organize sessions or discussions based on their shared interests and knowledge. Rather than a pre-planned schedule, the agenda emerges organically during the event, allowing for dynamic and engaging conversations.

What to Expect at the Unconference Day

  • Speed Geeking: Quickly learn and teach about diverse topics through rapid conversations with knowledgeable individuals. This fast-paced format allows you to explore a wide range of subjects in a short period.

  • Birds-of-a-Feather Sessions: Join or lead informal discussion groups centered around specific research interests, digital tools, or areas of expertise. These sessions foster organic conversations and networking opportunities among like-minded attendees.

  • Lightning Talks: Attend or present quick, five-minute presentations showcasing a variety of projects, research findings, or insights on digital tools. Lightning talks offer a rapid-fire way to expose yourself to numerous ideas and topics.

The Unconference Day is all about participation, collaboration, and sharing knowledge. Attendees can propose and lead sessions, contribute to discussions, or simply engage as active learners. The schedule will be dynamic, allowing you to join sessions that pique your curiosity and align with your interests.

Don’t miss this opportunity to shape your own ResBaz experience and connect with fellow researchers in an interactive and engaging environment!
